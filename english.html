Explore the life and work of Ila Sachani

Who is Ila Sachani?

Ila Sachani was once a farmer's daughter facing a big challenge, work very hard and became a celebrated artist. She even received the prestigious president's Medal, which was a huge honour. The most important for Ila was the new sense of independence she gained through her skills.

What is so special about Ila Sachani?

Ila was born with a deformity that rendered her arms useless. So, she taught her feet of making fine patterns in traditional embroidery.

How did the new independence help Ila?

The "new independence" gave Ila sense of confidence and freedom, allowing her to do things on her own. Even by the "new independence" Ila achieved through her embroidery skills not only transformed her economic status but made her happy and proud as well.

What does Ila's journey teaches us?

Ila's journey teaches us that determination, family support, and hard work can help overcome physical limitations. Her story is an inspiring journey.
