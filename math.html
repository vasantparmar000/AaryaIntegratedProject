Aryabhatta, an ancient Indian mathematicians and astronomer, was one of the most influential Scholars in the history of Indian mathematics and Science. He lived during the Gupta period in 5th century CE and is often regarded as the father of Indian astronomy and mathematics. Aryabhatta's remarkable contribution have left a lasting impact on the fields of mathematics, astronomy and timekeeping making a revered figure in the annals of human knowledge. Aryabhatta's most significant contribution to astronomy came in the farm of his magnum opus, the Aryabhatta.
